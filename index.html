    Logix — Smart time tracking for Keka attendance

    Logix is a lightweight Chrome extension that automates attendance logging in Keka. It eliminates manual entry, supports flexible schedules, and delivers accurate time tracking with one click.

  • Problem: manual Keka attendance is slow, repetitive, and error-prone.
  • Solution: one-click automation directly on the Keka attendance dashboard.
  • Impact: faster logging, fewer mistakes, and more reliable attendance records.

    • Key capabilities include multi-location support, lunch break deduction, real-time hour tracking, and a clean user experience that works immediately after install.
